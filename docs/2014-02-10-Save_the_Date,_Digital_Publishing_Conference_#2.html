Save the Date, Digital Publishing Conference #2

By margreet riphagen, February 10, 2014 at 10:27 am.

In May 22nd and 23rd the second event about Digital Publishing will be held in Rotterdam. The conference will take part in the Museum Boijmans van Beuningen. And various workshops, as well as the artistic evening program will take part in Worm.
