A reading device with some character and soul please

By haroldkonickx, October 14, 2014 at 4:01 pm.

Working in the field of digital media, designer and HVA lecturer Frank Kloos regretfully admits his love for physical books. Nevertheless he fantasizes about a reading device that is as good as or maybe even better than the real thing.
